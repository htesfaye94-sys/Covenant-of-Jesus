The Sacred Mission

The Covenant is not merely historical, but ontological.

“We bridge ancient Patristic wisdom with modern philosophical discourse to provide rigorous answers for seekers of Truth.”

Our primary mission is the intellectual preservation of the Oriental Orthodox faith (Tewahido). We do not offer mere devotional content, but structural theological defense against modern secularism and heterodoxy.

Scholastic Pillars

1. Patristics

Systematic study of the Early Church Fathers to ensure Apostolic continuity. We focus on the writings of St. Cyril, St. Severus, and the Desert Fathers.

2. Philosophy

Using Metaphysics and Logic to defend the rationality of the Incarnation. We engage with ontology, epistemology, and ethics from an Orthodox perspective.

3. Scripture

Exegesis through original languages (Ge'ez, Greek, Coptic) and authoritative interpretative lenses of the Holy Councils.
