The Sacred Mission

የተልዕኮ መግለጫ

መግለጫ

የዚህ ድረ-ገጽ ዋነኛ ዓላማ የጥንታዊቷን የኦሬንታል ኦርቶዶክስ ተዋሕዶ ቤተክርስቲያንን እምነትና ትምህርት በአእምሮአዊ ሙግት መከላከል ነው። ጥንታዊውን የአባቶች ጥበብ ከዘመናዊው የፍልስፍና ምርምር ጋር በማገናኘት ለእውነት ፈላጊዎች ሁሉ ጥልቅ ምላሽ ለመስጠት እንተጋለን።

Institutional Statement

The primary mission of this portal is the intellectual preservation and defense of the Oriental Orthodox faith. By bridging ancient Patristic wisdom with modern philosophical discourse, we provide rigorous scholarly answers for all seekers of Divine Truth. We operate under the standard of high academic rigor.

Scholarly Inquiry

ጥናትና ምርምር

ክሪስቶሎጂ

ትኩረታችን በዋናነት በታላቁ ቅዱስ ቄርሎስ እና በቅዱስ ሳዊሮስ ዘአንጾኪያ ነገረ ክርስቶስ (Christology) ላይ ያተኮረ ነው። በተጨማሪም የሜታ-ፊዚክስ እና የሎጂክ ትምህርቶችን በመጠቀም የፈጣሪን ህልውና እንመረምራለን።

Scholastic Method

Our research centers upon the high Christology of St. Cyril of Alexandria and St. Severus of Antioch. Utilizing the analytical frameworks of Metaphysics and Logic, we examine the necessary existence of the Godhead and the mystery of the Incarnate Logos through rigorous dialectical analysis.

The systematic study of the writings and teachings of the Early Church Fathers, ensuring the preservation of the Apostolic tradition against modern heterodoxy.
