The Sacred Mission

Our work is dedicated to the intellectual preservation and defense of the Oriental Orthodox faith. By synthesizing ancient Patristic wisdom with modern philosophical rigor, we seek to provide a sanctuary for those pursuing the depths of Divine Truth.

Academic Focus

Centering on the high Christology of St. Cyril of Alexandria and St. Severus of Antioch, our research explores the Miaphysite tradition. Through the lens of Metaphysics and Logic, we investigate the necessary existence of the Creator and the mystery of the Incarnate Logos.
