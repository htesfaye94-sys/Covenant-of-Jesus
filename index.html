The Sacred Mission

Our mission is the intellectual preservation and defense of the Oriental Orthodox faith. We bridge ancient Patristic wisdom with modern philosophical discourse, providing a sanctuary for rigorous inquiry and spiritual devotion.

Academic Pursuit

Dedicated to the study of the Church Fathers, specifically the Christology of St. Cyril of Alexandria and St. Severus of Antioch. Through the lens of Metaphysics and Philosophy, we explore the necessary existence of the Creator and the profound depths of the Incarnate Word.
