INSTITUTIONAL STATEMENT

The primary mission of this portal is the intellectual preservation and defense of the Oriental Orthodox faith. By bridging ancient Patristic wisdom with modern philosophical discourse, we provide rigorous scholarly answers for all seekers of Divine Truth. We operate under the standard of ecclesiastical rigor.
