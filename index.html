The Sacred Mission

The Covenant is not merely historical, but ontological.

“We bridge ancient Patristic wisdom with modern philosophical discourse to provide rigorous answers for seekers of Truth.”

Our primary mission is the intellectual preservation of the Oriental Orthodox faith. We do not offer mere devotional content, but structural theological defense.

Scholastic Pillars

1. Patristics

Systematic study of the Early Church Fathers to ensure Apostolic continuity.

2. Philosophy

Using Metaphysics and Logic to defend the rationality of the Incarnation.

3. Scripture

Exegesis through original languages and authoritative interpretative lenses.
