The Sacred Mission

The Covenant is not merely historical, but ontological. We bridge ancient Patristic wisdom with modern philosophical discourse to provide rigorous answers for seekers of Truth.

“Knowledge without communion is void; faith without reason is fragile. We seek the union of both in the mystery of the Incarnation.”

Our primary mission is the intellectual preservation of the Oriental Orthodox faith (Tewahido). We do not offer mere devotional content, but structural theological defense against modern secularism and heterodoxy.

Scholastic Pillars

The structural basis of our inquiry.

1. Patristics

The Fathers are not dead voices, but living guides.

Systematic study of the Early Church Fathers (St. Cyril, St. Severus, St. Dioscorus) to ensure Apostolic continuity and correct dogma.

2. Philosophy

Reason as the handmaiden of Revelation.

Using Metaphysics, Epistemology, and Logic to defend the rationality of the Incarnation and the necessity of the Divine.

3. Scripture

The breath of God in human language.

Exegesis through original languages (Ge'ez, Greek, Coptic) and authoritative interpretative lenses of the Holy Councils.

About This Archive

Maintainer: The Covenant Scholastic Society.

Purpose: To provide a digital sanctuary for rigorous theological inquiry.

Orientation: Strictly Oriental Orthodox (Non-Chalcedonian).

This is not a blog. It is a living repository of ancient truth, maintained for the edification of the faithful and the answer of the skeptic. We adhere to the decisions of the first three Ecumenical Councils: Nicaea, Constantinople, and Ephesus.
