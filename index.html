The Sacred Mission

Our mission is the intellectual preservation and defense of the Oriental Orthodox faith. We bridge ancient Patristic wisdom with modern philosophical discourse, providing a sanctuary for rigorous inquiry.

About

Dedicated to the study of the Church Fathers, specifically the Christology of St. Cyril of Alexandria and St. Severus of Antioch. We explore the necessary existence of the Creator and the depths of the Incarnate Word.
