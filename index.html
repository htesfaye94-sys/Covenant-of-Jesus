Statement

The core mission of this portal is the intellectual preservation and defense of the Oriental Orthodox faith. By bridging ancient Patristic wisdom with modern philosophical discourse, we seek to provide rigorous answers for all seekers of Divine Truth.

Scholastic Focus

ትኩረት

Philosophy & Theology

Our research centers on the high Christology of St. Cyril of Alexandria and St. Severus of Antioch. Through Metaphysics and Logic, we examine the necessary existence of the Godhead and the profound mystery of the Incarnate Logos.
